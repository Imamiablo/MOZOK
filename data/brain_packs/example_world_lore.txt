## ANIMALS

### Bobozavrik
Lives in the mountains, feeds on beans and humans. Looks like a pink brachiosaurus, but with a dull smile. Very friendly to those who give it beans.

### Werewolf
A nocturnal animal similar to a wolf, but with human eyes. Can transform into a human during a full moon. Afraid of silver and fire.

## PLANTS

### Dragon Leaf
Poisonous leaves from dark forests. Causes muscle paralysis upon contact. Used by alchemists for potions.
